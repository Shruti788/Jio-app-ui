Frequently Asked Questions

How do I recharge my Jio number?

You can recharge your Jio number through MyJio app, website, or at any retail store. Simply enter your mobile number, choose a plan, and complete the payment.

What is Jio 5G?

Jio 5G is India's fastest 5G network offering speeds up to 1 Gbps, low latency, and enhanced connectivity. It's available in 1000+ cities across India.

How can I port my number to Jio?

To port to Jio, send SMS PORT <10-digit mobile number> to 1900. You'll receive a UPC code. Visit a Jio store with the UPC code and required documents to complete the porting process.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major payment methods including Credit/Debit cards, Net Banking, UPI, Paytm, PhonePe, Google Pay, and digital wallets.

How do I check my data balance?

Dial *333# or download the MyJio app to check your data balance, validity, and other account details instantly.
